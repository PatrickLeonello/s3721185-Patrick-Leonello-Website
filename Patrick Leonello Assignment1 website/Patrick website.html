Project Idea 

overview

My Project idea is an application called "Suggest". This Application's core idea is to create a program that allows you to suggest popular/niche Movies, music, TV shows, books, Games and more to friends, family or Randoms quickly and easily with a swipe of the finger. The Application will have a minimalistic design to come across straightforward and not overwhelming. The idea stems from constantly being suggested TV shows in which I forget to follow up on or don't know where to stream. The final Application will be as easy as Search, Swipe suggest! The user gets reminders on recommended media and shows available platforms to stream/purchase them.

Motivation

Yes, Most websites and streaming services have the share button; however, according to Moovweb.com, who studied 61 million mobile sessions and discovered the "99.8% of mobile users never engage with social media share buttons". Showing there is a high level of engagement but next to no sharing to others. I believe that this is because there isn't any incentive to use a share button. It's simply not satisfying and might even come across as annoying to whom you're sharing. This is where the Suggest app comes in. It will be an utterly simple, enjoyable and rewarding experience for both parties with added graphics and serotonin inducing visuals and feedback to share your favourite media.

Description

Initially, my interest in IT solely lies in UCD (user-centred design)in the creation of applications and websites and how combining different design elements and principles such as contrast, colour, typefaces can all affect how the user interacts and feels and how that can influence their experience in positive and negative ways.

 The application theme will have a simple atmospheric dream-like quality to it with soft rounded and colourful bubble avatars/options, particle effects and almost liquid-like animations and graphics to keep it friendly and from overwhelming users. The app's audio will be at a minimum with only positive feedback sounds for pressing buttons sounding like soft pops and water droplets, and when sending a suggestion, a slight soft whistle noise. As the aim is to reward the users for every suggestion, the idea is to make the application a gratifying and satisfying experience to use. The inclusion of vibration feedback with every press will help this.

 The user will be able to select from different modes. These include the primary friend to friend suggestion mode and a mode where the user will be paired with people who suggest similar things (this way, the suggestion will more likely please both) and they both must suggest to each other at the same time. Group suggest mode where the users can form a watch group where you can send suggestions to a whole group of 3-6 friends. Finally, a mode where you are paired with a random with different or unexpected likes compared to you. That way, the user can broaden their media intake horizon. The users gained suggestions will be inserted into a section of the app that will provide information such as the date it was suggested, streaming/purchasing services available to view the media and which friend/user suggested it.

 To follow the theme of satisfaction, when a user completes viewing, listening or experiencing a suggestion made to them, they can 'pop' the bubble and are rewarded with a large amount of positive visual and audible feedback. They can then rate the suggestion based on how much they enjoyed it and leave a message to the user who originally suggested it. They can quickly suggest it to another person right there if desired.

 To make the user feel more in control most colours and avatars will be customizable.

 Restrictions will also be a part of the options in the applications for example a mode that restricts explicit content suggestions (Family mode.). Another feature will be a mute suggestions option if you feel someone is suggesting too often.

To build the app learning code such as Swift and using Xcode to edit code and debug. While a lot of the core learning can be completed in the bachelor of information technology there are countless online short courses, website tutorials and youtube videos on the internet to gain the knowledge and skills needed to create the app

Outcome

Communities are built, and relationships strengthened when people can share, laugh, cry and relate to each other through mutual interest. The app Suggest can successfully bring people together through the sharing of everyday media intake. The share button was initially created so people could send suggestions to friends and family. People outside the digital world love to share! Also, it’s free advertising which is why on paper the share button is perfect; however, no one uses it. Why? Because there is no satisfaction, reward or incentive to do so. The application of Suggest will bring that much-needed gratification and a sense of community to the market.
